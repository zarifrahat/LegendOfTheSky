Legend of the Sky

Mother: "What bedtime story do you want to hear tonight sweetheart?"
Child: "My favorite one! About the legend of the sky!"
Mother: "Okay, but only if you brushed your teeth like you were supposed to."
Child: "Mooooom! I did!"
Mother: "Now where do I start..."
Child: "On the third year of the great war..."
Mother: "Oh yes, thats how your dad usually starts it, isn't that right? Well, on the third year of the greatest war, a brave squadron flew behind enemy lines to gather intelligence. The journey inward was easy, suspiciously easy. However, the pilots squashed any ominous gut feelings they had because they wanted the mission to succeed. When the information was gathered and our brave men and women began to fly back, they were ambushed! During the hardfought battle in the sky, our planes were hit again and again. One by one they plummeted in brilliant explosions of red, orange, and black against the cold, blue, and white sky. One after another they fell, until there was but one left. And what happened after that is the stuff of myths and legends..."
